## What is shaduxe/ui?

shaduxe/ui is a carefully curated collection of powerful variations built on top of shadcn/ui, designed to take your UI to the next level.

### Key Features

- **Built on shadcn/ui**: Leverages the power and flexibility of shadcn/ui components.
- **Easy Installation**: All components are easily installable via the familiar shadcn CLI, but from our custom registry.
- **Powerful Variations**: Extend your design possibilities with expertly crafted component variants.

## Getting Started

The best part? If you're familiar with shadcn/ui, you already know how to use shaduxe/ui! We use the exact same installation process as shadcn/ui, just with our custom registry.

## Why shaduxe/ui?

1. **Seamless Integration**: Works perfectly with your existing shadcn/ui setup.
2. **Time-Saving**: Pre-built variations mean less time styling, more time building.
3. **Flexible**: Like shadcn/ui, the code is yours to customize and adapt.
4. **Expanded Design Options**: More variants out of the box for each component.

## How It Works

1. **Choose**: Select the shaduxe/ui component you want to use.
2. **Install**: Use the shadcn CLI to add the component to your project from our registry.
3. **Import**: Bring the component and its variations into your project.
4. **Customize**: Tailor the components to your specific needs, just like with shadcn/ui.
